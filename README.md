Tools I Used

- Power BI Desktop – Built the dashboard and all the visuals
- Microsoft Excel – For initial checking and cleaning

Key Numbers from the Dashboard

- Total Revenue: 551.25K
- Total Appointments: 200
- Total Patients: 50
- Collection Rate: 31% (This is very low)
- No-Show Rate: 26%

Main Insights

- The hospital is only collecting 31% of the money they bill. This is a big problem.
- 26% of patients are not showing up for their appointments.
- Doctor Sarah handled the most appointments.
- Highest patient registrations happened in : April
- Patients Gender : 62% male while  38% female
- Most patients visited the hospital for Consultation
- Highest appoinment Month : September 
- Most patients use Insurance, but collection from insurance is poor.

My Business Recommendations

1. Improve Payment Collection
   Start sending automatic reminders (SMS or Email) for pending and failed payments. This can help increase collection rate quickly.

2. Reduce No-Shows
   Send appointment confirmations and reminders one day before. Goal is to bring no-show rate below 15%.

3. Doctor Workload
   Give more appointments to strong doctors like Sarah and Jane. Provide support to doctors who have fewer appointments.

4. Payment Methods
   Encourage more Cash and Credit Card payments by giving small discounts. Reduce too much dependency on Insurance.

5. Patient Loyalty
   Start a simple loyalty program for regular patients and promote Consultation & Checkup services more.

This project helped me practice SQL, data modeling in Power BI, and how to turn data into useful business suggestions.
